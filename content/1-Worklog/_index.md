**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Cloud Computing Overview, AWS Ecosystem, and Essential Tools](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Deep dive into IAM, VPC Networking, and Amazon EC2](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Amazon S3, AWS Lambda, and Serverless Computing](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Amazon DynamoDB, API Gateway, and Serverless Architecture](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Amazon RDS, ElastiCache, and Database Migration](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [System Monitoring with CloudWatch and DNS with Route 53](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Group Project Kickoff – Business, Booking & Admin Service: Requirements Analysis](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [System Architecture Design & Database Modeling](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Business Owner Features: Dashboard, Claim Listing & My Business Listings](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Booking Features: Booking Page & My Bookings](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Admin Features: Dashboard, User/Place/Report Management](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Testing, Polish & Final Submission](1.12-week12/)
