# Event 2 Summary

During the event, the speakers shared insights on a wide range of topics.

**Hoàng Thái Linh** introduced ways to learn AWS through AWS Cloud Quest and the Floci tool. This hands-on approach makes AWS more accessible to beginners while helping them reduce the risk of unexpected costs.

**Nguyễn Trần Minh Quân, An Khương Huỳnh, and Quốc Anh Mai** shared their experience participating in LotusHacks 2026. The team presented how they built projects such as SynthHunter and Vortex, taking them from initial ideas to minimum viable products (MVPs) within a short period.

**Nguyễn Thị Quỳnh Như** spoke about confidence in learning and at work. Her talk helped attendees understand that confidence does not mean being free from fear; it means being willing to try even when they still feel worried.

**Trần Nghĩa** presented the Tử Vi Đại Việt application and its migration to AWS. This session helped me better understand serverless architecture, managed services, and cost optimization for startups.

**Trần Minh Quân** discussed DevOps and the hidden issues that can arise in projects. He emphasized that DevOps is not only about tools, but also about people, processes, and effective teamwork.

**Phạm Khắc Uy** spoke about procrastination. His talk showed that procrastination is not always caused by laziness; it can also stem from fear of failure, fear of judgment, or a lack of confidence.
