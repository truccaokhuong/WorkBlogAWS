### Business & Admin Flow

This section demonstrates the screens for business owners and administrators in Travel Platform. The features focus on business operation management, place verification requests, and system overview.

#### 1. Business Owner Dashboard

Business owners use the dashboard to monitor information related to the places/services they manage.

![Business owner dashboard](/images/demo/demo-11-business-dashboard.png)

#### 2. Place Verification Request

Business owners can submit a place verification request to connect a place with the appropriate management account.

![Business owner submits a place verification request](/images/demo/demo-12-business-claim.png)

#### 3. Administrator Dashboard

Administrators use the overview dashboard to monitor system status, requests, and operational data.

![Administrator overview dashboard](/images/demo/demo-13-admin-dashboard.png)
